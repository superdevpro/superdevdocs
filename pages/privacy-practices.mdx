# Privacy Practices

At SuperDev Pro, we prioritize your privacy. Our commitment is reflected in our minimal data collection practices and transparent policies. We believe in providing a powerful web development tool without compromising personal privacy.

## Data Collection

- When you purchase with us, we collect your name, email, country, timestamp, quantity, and amount.
- When you activate the extension, we collect your user-agent and timestamp for the device management feature.
- We do not collect any other information on our product and website apart from what's mentioned on this page.

## 3rd Party Services

- Our website is hosted on [Vercel](https://vercel.com), which is compliant with GDPR, and HIPAA.
- Our database is hosted on [Neon](https://neon.tech), which is compliant with GDPR, and SOC 2.
- We use [Plausible](https://plausible.io) analytics, which is compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR.
- We use [Stripe](https://stripe.com), [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com), and [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com) for payment processing.

## Other Measures

- Our website maintains a strict no-cookie policy, ensuring your interactions remain private and cookie-free.
- We use static code analysis tools to find and fix bugs/vulnerabilities during our software development cycle.
- We push software updates, patches, and security fixes multiple times monthly for our product and website.
