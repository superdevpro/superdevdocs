# Privacy Practices

At SuperDev Pro, we prioritize your privacy. Our commitment is reflected in our minimal data collection practices and transparent policies. We believe in providing a powerful web development tool without compromising personal privacy.

1. When you purchase with us, we collect your name, email, country, timestamp, quantity, and amount.
2. When you activate the extension, we collect your user agent and timestamp for the device management feature.
3. Our websites maintain a strict no-cookie policy, ensuring your interactions remain private and cookie-free.
4. We do not collect logs on our website or product and use GDPR, CCPA compliant Plausible analytics.
5. We use static code analysis tools to find and fix bugs/vulnerabilities during our software development cycle.
6. We push software updates, patches, and security fixes multiple times a month for our products and websites.
