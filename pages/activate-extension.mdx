# Activate Extension

Activate your SuperDev Pro extension to unlock its full range of features. Follow the instructions below based on your installation status:

## New Install

1. Install the SuperDev Pro extension, [instructions here](/install-extension).
2. Once installed, open the SuperDev Pro extension.
3. Enter your license key into the popup window.
4. Click the `Activate` button to complete the activation.

## Pro Upgrade

1. Open the SuperDev Pro extension.
2. Click the settings icon in the extension's navbar.
3. Scroll down to the bottom of the settings page.
4. Click on the `Click to Activate License` button.
5. Enter your license key into the popup window.
6. Click the `Activate` button to complete the activation.
