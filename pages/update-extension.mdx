# Update Extension

{findBrowser()} updates your extensions automatically, but sometimes it can take a while for these updates to be applied. To ensure your SuperDev Pro extension is up-to-date, follow these instructions:

1. Go to <code>{findBrowser().toLowerCase()}://extensions</code> to open the extensions page.
2. Enable `Developer Mode` from the top right corner.
3. Click on the `Update` button from the top left corner to update.
4. Your SuperDev Pro extension should now be updated.
