# Take Screenshot

Take Screenshot feature lets you capture visible area, custom area, or full page screenshot of any website in PNG, JPEG, or PDF format. Note that saving in PDF may take some time, so please be patient.

![Take Screenshot](/features/take-screenshot.png)

## Use Cases

- **Bug Reporting**: Capture screenshots to document visual issues or bugs.
- **Content Archiving**: Take screenshots for archival purposes or for offline viewing.
- **Design Review**: Use screenshots to review or compare design changes over time.
- **Sharing Information**: Quickly share what you see on your screen with colleagues or clients.
- **Documentation**: Include screenshots in your technical documentation for more comprehensive guides.
