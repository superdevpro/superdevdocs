# Fonts Changer

Fonts Changer empowers you to experiment with over 1100+ Google fonts, categorized into sans-serif, serif, monospace, display, local, and handwriting types. You have the freedom to apply these fonts to your entire website or to paragraphs or headings. Furthermore, you can save these changes to the extension storage, copy them to your clipboard, or export them for other projects.

![Fonts Changer](/features/fonts-changer.png)

## Use Cases

- **Website Redesign**: Quickly experiment with different fonts to find the perfect fit for a new look.
- **Accessibility**: Opt for fonts that enhance the readability of your website, making it more accessible.
- **Personal Customization**: Customize your favorite websites by changing their default fonts to ones you prefer.
